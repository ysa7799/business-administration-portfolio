# Shift Handover & Incident Escalation — SOP

A professional **Standard Operating Procedure** documenting how a shift is handed over and how incidents are escalated by severity. Written to the format used in real operations/compliance environments.

## What's inside (`Shift_Handover_SOP.docx` / `.pdf`)
- Purpose, scope, and definitions
- Roles & responsibilities (operator, supervisor, duty manager)
- Step-by-step handover procedure
- **Incident escalation matrix** (Severity S1–S4 → who to notify → timeframe)
- Records/compliance retention rules and a revision-history table

## Why it matters
Clear SOPs reduce errors, speed up escalation, and create an audit trail — core to operations, administration, and facilities roles.

## Skills demonstrated
Process documentation, SOP authoring, RACI-style ownership, escalation design, compliance/records awareness, professional business writing.
